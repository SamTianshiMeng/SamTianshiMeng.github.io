My Interests



Olympic Shooting

I was introduced to Olympic Shooting while participating in the Royal Canadian Air Cadets, I thoroughly enjoyed the experience as it taught me to remain calm under stressful situations, as well as providing an effect similar to mediation as it allowed me to collect my thoughts. Since I was really passionate about Olympic Shooting I want to shoot for the stars aim for Olympic Champion. In which my journey started when i competed in a regional competition with a cadet rifle while everyone had olympic level rifle and despite the disadvantage I was middle of the pack and this strange behavior caught the eye of my now coach Pat Vamplew (former Olympian). After getting to know me he determined that I have the potiental to succeed and gave me my initial gears and my first olympic rifle in which I won a regional competition with 3 month of training then provincial championships 5 month later. Finally I won nationals in 2022 after training for 2 years, but due to a recent injury, I did not qualify for the pan american games.

For games I was a avid starcraft 2 player when I was young due to the lack of social interaction within the game as it is mostly a 1v1 game therefore I transition to league of legends where I have reached diamond a rank where I could tryout for the esport team at Toronto Metroplitan university.
